Before you ask

Quick questions, answered.

Is this a subscription or a contract?
No. The £75 pack is one-off, no strings. The £150/month option exists if you want it kept up, but there's no contract — cancel any time, for any reason.
What if I don't like the report?
You don't pay. You see the full thing first — there's no awkward conversation, no chasing, no obligation either way.
Do I need to do anything technical?
No. You send your business name and town by email, I do the research, you get a link to read. Posting the content is the only "doing" involved, and even that's optional.
How is this different to an agency?
No retainer, no minimum term, no account manager — just one person doing the research properly, once, for a fair price. If that's not enough for what you need, an agency might genuinely be the better fit, and that's fine.
How long does it actually take?
Your finished report within 48 hours of sending your details. I take on a handful at a time so each one gets proper attention rather than being rushed.
