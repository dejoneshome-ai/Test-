What these three links actually show you

Google search is what most new customers do first — if your website or listing isn't in the first 3 results, a competitor is getting found instead of you.

Google Maps shows your review count and how recently you got one. This is usually the single biggest gap between businesses that look "alive" online and ones that look forgotten.

Facebook search shows how long it's been since you last posted. Anything over a month reads as "maybe closed" to someone who's never visited before.

That's everything you can check yourself in a couple of minutes for your business. What it doesn't show you: which specific competitors are ahead of you and why, what they're posting that you're not, and what to actually do about it this week. That's exactly what the full pack below covers.
